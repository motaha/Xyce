Detailed Description

Rate constant calculator for Electron or Hole capture reaction.

These reactions are of the form R + E -> P; electron_capture( ) or R+H->P; hole_capture( ). The reaction rate is then , where v is 2.3e7 for electron capture and 1.9e7 for holes.
